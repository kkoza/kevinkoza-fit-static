The 6 Week Foundation

A video-based course with weekly lessons, guided workouts, and practical assignments designed to build sustainable fitness habits

Week 1-2: Building the Foundation

Start with bodyweight exercises you can do anywhere. Learn fundamental movement patterns and begin establishing your exercise routine. By week two, we'll introduce equipment recommendations as you prepare for progressive overload.

Week 3-4: Adding Resistance

Incorporate weighted exercises starting with a single kettlebell. Learn proper form, understand training principles, and develop the mindset needed for long-term consistency. These weeks focus on building both physical strength and mental resilience.

Week 5-6: Gym Confidence & Beyond

Transition to gym training or continue progressing at home—your choice. Build familiarity with gym equipment, overcome any remaining anxiety, and walk away with a complete understanding of how to continue your fitness journey independently.

Each week includes fitness principles, mindset lessons, guided workouts, and actionable assignments

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you want to start building muscle, but you don't know where to begin?

Maybe you have some familiarity with exercise, but you want a structured routine which shows you exactly what to do to make progress.

Maybe you'd like to get started with fitness, but you have some anxiety around the gym and you haven't solidified the foundational habits to exercise.

Whether you want to find the right way to work out at home, or you'd like to build confidence in the gym, this course is for you.

Over 6 weeks, you will:

  • Learn a muscle-building at-home routine which you can optionally extend into the gym.
  • Shift your identity towards being a person who prioritizes fitness and align your actions with that identity.
  • Walk away with the mindset that exercise is not intimidating, and is inherently rewarding, for both the act itself and the long-term health outcomes.
  • Learn the strategies to tap into your motivations, build consistency, and leverage momentum in your fitness practice.
  • Understand key weight training principles which you can use to extend your fitness practice.
  • Build familiarity and confidence in the gym.
