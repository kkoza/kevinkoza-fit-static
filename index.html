Why Most Programs Don't Work

I think the fitness industry has this backwards. Most programs try to keep you dependent—endless subscriptions, new "secret methods," constant hand-holding. I don't want that for you, and honestly, I don't think it's effective long-term.

  • You need someone who understands your specific situation, not another cookie-cutter template
  • You need accountability on the days when motivation disappears (and it will)
  • You need someone to catch your form issues before they become chronic problems
  • You need to learn the WHY behind everything, not just what exercises to do

I'm a firm believer that you're capable of learning to manage your own fitness. You just need someone to show you how the system works, then give you practice applying it. That's what this program does.

How We'll Work Together

I've structured this as three distinct phases, each building on the last. The aim is to move you from dependence to independence over 90 days.

Month One: Building the Foundation

  • We start with a custom program built around your goals, available equipment, and schedule constraints
  • Daily text check-ins where I respond to everything—this is about building the habit first
  • Weekly 30-minute calls where we troubleshoot what's working and what isn't
  • Access to my private Discord where you can ask questions and connect with others in the program

Month Two: Understanding Progressive Overload

  • We adjust your program based on YOUR actual progress, not some predetermined template
  • Form check videos so you're lifting safely and effectively (this alone prevents most injuries)
  • Nutrition guidance that fits your lifestyle—I'm not going to guilt trip you about meal prep
  • You start understanding the principles: why we're doing what we're doing

Month Three: Becoming Independent

  • I teach you how to program for yourself—the principles of progressive overload, volume, and recovery
  • We build your plan for maintaining results after our 90 days end
  • Final assessment and your roadmap for continued progress
  • You graduate understanding how to adapt programs to your needs without a coach
Success looks like this: You finish the program with visible progress AND the knowledge to keep improving without me. That's what I'm aiming for.
